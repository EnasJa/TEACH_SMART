About Us

The Problem

In many schools, teachers face significant gaps in the learning levels of students within a single classroom. Managing a wide range of learning levels and personal progress creates substantial challenges in classroom management and curriculum adaptation.

The problem consists of several main components:

  • Early identification of learning difficulties: Teachers struggle to identify students with learning difficulties in real-time. These students do not receive the necessary support at the appropriate time.
  • Continuous monitoring of student progress: It is difficult for teachers to continuously track each student's progress. Furthermore, the feedback provided to students is not always accurate and effective.

Our Solution

We offer an AI-based system that helps teachers identify students' learning difficulties in real-time and continuously monitor their progress.

  • Early identification of learning difficulties using machine learning algorithms.
  • Continuous monitoring of student progress and providing accurate and effective feedback.
  • Adapting the curriculum to match each student's learning level.

Our Team

Our team includes experts in artificial intelligence, experienced teachers, and education specialists. Together, we work to create innovative solutions that enhance the learning experience for students and improve teaching quality in schools.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a learning environment where every student receives the necessary support according to their individual needs. We believe that technology can improve the learning experience and help teachers perform their work more efficiently and effectively.
